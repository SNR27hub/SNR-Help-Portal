Dhyan Dein: Agar kisi user dwara galat jankari (Wrong Information), fake reports, ya kisi bhi tarah ka galat kaam is platform par kiya jata hai, toh uske liye **SNR Team, Developer, ya SNR brand** bilkul bhi zimmedar (Responsible) nahi honge. SNR Team ki koi dosh (Fault) nahi hogi agar data ka galat istemal hota hai.
